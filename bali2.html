Discover The Best Of Bali 5 Nights / 6 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

  • Pick up from the airport and transfer to the hotel.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local Indian restaurant(Dinner included)

Day 2: Full-Day Kintamani Volcano Tour

  • After breakfast, the tour starts at 09:00 AM.
  • Visit Batubulan Village to see traditional handmade Batik painting.
  • Explore Mas Village to experience Balinese painting and wood carving.
  • Continue to a highland coffee plantation, where you'll learn about different types of coffee, including the famous Luwak Coffee and authentic Balinese coffee.
  • Enjoy coffee tasting on site.
  • The highlight of the day: scenic views of Mount Batur and Lake Batur.
  • Indonesian lunch will be served at a local restaurant with volcano views.
  • On the return trip, stop at the spectacular Tegalalang Rice Terraces.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local Indian restaurant(Lunch & Dinner included).

Day 3: Water Sports & Uluwatu Temple Tour

  • After breakfast, depart from the hotel at 09:00 AM.
  • Head to Tanjung Benoa Beach for exciting water sports activities.
  • Package includes one round of Banana Boat ride.
  • Optional water sports (at personal expense) include: adventure parasailing, jet ski, flying fish, snorkeling, sky tube, sea walker, diving, and more.
  • Lunch: Indian set meal at a local Indian restaurant in Nusa Dua.
  • After lunch, proceed to the Uluwatu Temple Tour.
  • Visit the ancient Hindu temple perched on a dramatic cliff over 100 meters above sea level.
  • Enjoy the breathtaking ocean views and sunset.
  • Observe the sacred monkeys believed to guard the temple grounds.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local Indian restaurant(Lunch & Dinner included).

Day 4: Full-Day Trip to Nusa Penida (West Island Tour)

  • Early morning pick-up at 06:15 AM from the hotel (breakfast box provided).
  • Transfer to Sanur Beach to board a fast boat to Nusa Penida(Boat departs at 08:30 AM and arrives at Toyapakeh Pier around 09:30 AM).
  • Start the West Nusa Penida Island tour, visiting:(Angel's Billabong – A stunning natural infinity pool (photo stop only; no swimming allowed), Broken Beach (Pantai Pasih Uug) – A scenic coastal formation and natural arch (photo stop only; no swimming allowed), Kelingking Beach Viewpoint – The most iconic cliff view resembling a T-Rex (view from the top only; access to beach and swimming prohibited), Crystal Bay – Relax and swim in the clear waters; great spot for snorkeling (bring swimwear).).
  • Complimentary Indonesian set lunch at a local restaurant (1 menu item per person).
  • Return to Buyuk Pier to catch the fast boat back to Bali.
  • Evening: Dinner at a local Indian restaurant.
  • Note: Transportation on Nusa Penida will be via Suzuki APV, Toyota Avanza, or Daihatsu Xenia only.(Lunch & Dinner included).

Day 5: Taman Ayun, Bedugul Lake & Tanah Lot Tour

  • After breakfast, depart from the hotel at 09:00 AM.
  • Visit Taman Ayun Temple – the Royal Family temple located in Mengwi Village, known for its beautiful gardens and traditional Balinese architecture.
  • Continue to Ulun Danu Beratan Temple – a spectacular Hindu temple located on Lake Beratan, famous for its serene setting and floating illusion.
  • Indonesian lunch will be served at a local restaurant.
  • After lunch, visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, a sea temple set dramatically on a rock in the ocean(Weather permitting, enjoy a breathtaking sunset view at Tanah Lot).
  • Evening: Dinner at a local Indian restaurant(Lunch & Dinner included)

Day 6: Departure from Bali

  • After breakfast, free time at your own leisure.
  • Check-out and transfer to the airport for your departure flight(No meals included).
