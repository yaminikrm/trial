Discover The Best Of Bali 5 Nights / 6 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali

  • Pick up at the airport + welcome flower garland & transfer to Lunch at INDIAN restaurant. check in process at hotel. Evening : Dinner at Indian local restaurant (L.D).

Day 2: Full-Day Kintamani Volcano Tour

  • After breakfast, Tour starts at 09.00am : Full day KINTAMANI VOLCANO Tour, visit Traditional handmade BATIK PAINTING in Batubulan Village, visit PAINTING and WOOD CARVING at MAS village, continue to TEGENUNGAN WATERFALL in a lush jungle setting, a real natural view where visitors can relax, enjoy shallow fresh water pool next to a temple beside the river. Reaching down to the waterfall is only a several minute walk down some steps where visitors can take some pictures from different angles as visitors will enjoy the impressively green valley panorama with cascading water from the waterfall itself. The Highlight is to see a nice view of MOUNT BATUR & and its sister LAKE BATUR + Indonesian Lunch will be served at the local restaurant, on the way back will stop to view spectacular rice terraces in TEGALALANG Village. visit to UBUD HANDICRAFT VILLAGE. Shopping places are available on this tour and You may spend your time shopping for any kind of Balinese handicraft products good for souvenirs and or for collection. Evening : Dinner at Indian local restaurant (L.D).
  • Tour Highlights.
  • Batubulan Village: Witness the intricate process of handmade Batik painting, a renowned Balinese textile art passed down through generations.
  • Mas Village: Explore this artisan hub famous for traditional Balinese painting and exquisite wood carving, created by master craftsmen.
  • Tegenungan Waterfall: Set in a lush jungle landscape, this natural beauty spot offers a chance to relax beside a freshwater pool and riverside temple. A short walk down a staircase provides stunning panoramic views of the green valley and cascading waterfall—perfect for photography from multiple angles.
  • Mount Batur & Lake Batur: The highlight of the day is a stop at Kintamani, offering breathtaking views of the active Mount Batur volcano and its serene sister lake, Lake Batur.
  • Midday – Indonesian Lunch: Enjoy an authentic Indonesian lunch at a local restaurant overlooking the spectacular volcanic scenery.
  • Afternoon Stops.
  • Tegalalang Rice Terraces: Admire the world-famous rice terraces of Tegalalang, known for their dramatic layered layout and photogenic vistas.
  • Ubud Handicraft Village: Explore Ubud’s artisan scene with a visit to its handicraft village, where you’ll find a wide range of Balinese products ideal for souvenirs and keepsakes—from wood sculptures and textiles to silver jewelry and home décor.
  • Evening – Dinner: End your enriching day with a delicious Indian dinner at a local restaurant, followed by return to your hotel.

Day 3: Water Sports & Uluwatu Temple Tour

  • 09:00 AM – Departure from Hotel.
  • After breakfast, depart for an exciting morning at Tanjung Benoa Beach, Bali’s premier water sports destination.
  • Head to Tanjung Benoa Beach for exciting water sports activities.
  • Water Sports Activities.
  • Enjoy one round of Banana Boat ride—a fun and safe water activity perfect for all ages.
  • You may also choose from a variety of optional water sports available on-site at your own expense, such as: (Parasailing. Jet Skiing. Flying Fish. Snorkeling. Sky Tube. Sea Walker. Scuba Diving).
  • Noon – Lunch at an Indian Restaurant
  • Recharge with a flavorful Indian lunch before continuing the afternoon tour.
  • Afternoon – Uluwatu Temple Tour.
  • Visit the majestic Uluwatu Temple, one of Bali’s most iconic sea temples, perched dramatically on a 100-meter-high cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean.
  • Marvel at the splashing waves beneath the cliff and the stunning panoramic sunset views (weather permitting).
  • The temple is home to a group of sacred monkeys, believed by locals to be spiritual guardians of the temple grounds.
  • Evening – Dinner.
  • End the day with a hearty dinner at a local Indian restaurant, followed by a return transfer to your hotel.
  • Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner (L, D).

Day 4: Full-Day Trip to Nusa Penida (West Island Tour)

  • Early Morning – Transfer to Sanur Beach. After breakfast, head to Sanur Beach to catch a public fast boat to the enchanting Nusa Penida Island.
  • Boat departs at 07:30 AM / 08:30 AM. Arrive at Toyapakeh Pier around 09:30 AM. (Round-trip fast boat tickets included).
  • Island Sightseeing Tour Begins.
  • Angel’s Billabong: Explore this natural infinity pool with crystal-clear waters and mesmerizing rock formations—one of Nusa Penida’s most iconic hidden gems.
  • Broken Beach (Pantai Pasih Uug): Just a short walk from Angel’s Billabong, this spectacular coastal formation is the collapsed remains of a giant sea cave, forming a natural arch over turquoise waters—a perfect photo stop.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a complimentary Indonesian set lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Afternoon Highlights.
  • Kelingking Beach Viewpoint: One of the most photographed locations in Bali, this cliffside view resembles the shape of a T-Rex head. Enjoy scenic photo stops from the top only. (Access to the beach and swimming are not permitted for safety reasons.).
  • Bubu Beach: Wind down your afternoon at this secluded beach, ideal for swimming and snorkeling in its clear, calm waters. Don’t forget to bring your swimwear to fully enjoy the experience.
  • Return to Bali: Depart from Buyuk Pier by fast boat back to Sanur Beach.
  • Evening – Dinner: Upon return to the mainland, enjoy a relaxing Indian dinner at a local restaurant, followed by hotel transfer.
  • Note: The vehicle used on Nusa Penida Island will be Suzuki APV, Toyota Avanza, or Daihatsu Xenia, subject to availability. We recommend bringing sunblock, hat, camera, water bottle, and swimwear.
  • Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner (L, D).

Day 5: Taman Ayun, Bedugul Lake & Tanah Lot Tour

  • 09:00 AM – Departure from Hotel. After breakfast, embark on a full-day cultural and scenic tour exploring Bali’s most iconic temples and natural beauty.
  • Tour Highlights.
  • Taman Ayun Temple – Mengwi Village: Visit the stunning Taman Ayun Temple, a Royal Family temple of the Mengwi Kingdom, surrounded by beautiful gardens and moats. This UNESCO-recognized site reflects classic Balinese architectural beauty and spiritual heritage.
  • Ulun Danu Beratan Temple – Bedugul Highlands: Continue to the highlands of Bedugul to see the breathtaking Ulun Danu Temple, a floating Hindu temple set on Lake Beratan. Surrounded by misty mountains and cool weather, this is one of Bali’s most photogenic landmarks.
  • Midday – Lunch: Enjoy a delicious Indonesian set lunch at a local restaurant near the lake with serene panoramic views.
  • Afternoon Stop.
  • Tanah Lot Temple: End your sightseeing at Tanah Lot, Bali’s most iconic sea temple perched dramatically on a rock formation in the Indian Ocean. During high tide, it appears as though the temple is floating on water—a truly magical sunset spot and one of the most spiritually significant sea temples in Bali.
  • Evening – Dinner: Wrap up the day with a flavorful Indian dinner at a local restaurant, followed by transfer back to the hotel.
  • Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner (L, D).
