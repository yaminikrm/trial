Discover The Best Of Vietnam 3 Nights / 4 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival – Airport Transfer (No Guide)

  • Arrive in Vietnam – a beautiful country in Southeast Asia.
  • Meet our representative at the airport.
  • Transfer to your hotel located in the city center.
  • Hotel check-in time is from 14:00 onwards..
  • Rest of the day is free at leisure.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Da Nang - Marble Mountain – Hoi An (B/D) – Join Tour

  • Have breakfast at the hotel.
  • Free time in the morning.(Note: Marble Mountain – Hoi An tour starts in the afternoon, as Hoi An is especially beautiful at night.)
  • our guide will pick you up from your hotel.
  • Visit Marble Mountain – a cluster of five marble and limestone hills located in Ngu Hanh Son District, south of Da Nang City.
  • Continue to Hoi An Ancient Town and explore:(Japanese Bridge – the only remaining trace of Japanese culture, now a symbol of Hoi An, Take a walk along the Hoai River, soaking in the magical atmosphere of Hoi An at night, Admire the colorful lanterns hanging on houses, streets, and paths – creating a peaceful and serene ambiance.).
  • Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.
  • Return to your hotel and stay overnight.
  • Meals: Breakfast, Vietnamese Dinner.
  • Transfer: Join tour with guide in the afternoon.

Day 3: Bana Hill – Golden Bridge Hands (B/L) – Join Tour

  • Morning: Have breakfast at the hotel.
  • Our guide and driver will pick you up from your hotel.
  • Depart for Ba Na Hill via one of the most scenic seaside roads in Central Vietnam.
  • Upon arrival at Ba Na Hill: (Experience the cable car ride to the top, enjoying views of Mo Stream and Toc Tien Waterfall, Visit the Golden Bridge – held by the iconic "Hands of the Gods".).
  • Explore the L’Jardin Zone with French architecture: (Tinh Tâm Garden, French Stable, A 100-year-old pomelo tree, Old villas, Linh Ung Pagoda, 27-meter Buddha Statue, Loc Uyen Garden).
  • Afternoon: (Take the second cable car from Debay Station to Nui Chua Peak, the rooftop of Da Nang, Enjoy a buffet lunch on Ba Na Hill, Visit Fantasy Park – one of Asia’s most modern indoor amusement centers, Explore Linh Chua Linh Tu Temple and enjoy a panoramic view of Da Nang City and Bay from the peak).
  • Descend by cable car.
  • Return to the city center by coach, arriving.
  • End of the tour.
  • Meals: Breakfast, Buffet Lunch.
  • Inclusions: Round-trip Ba Na Hill Cable Car.

Day 4: Departure – Airport Transfer (B) – No Guide

  • Have breakfast at the hotel.
  • Free time for shopping or walking around the city.
  • Enjoy leisure time until your transfer to the International Airport for departure.
  • Goodbye Vietnam!
  • Meals: Breakfast.
  • Transfer: Private car for airport transfer (without guide).
