Discover The Best Of Almaty 6 Nights / 7 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day-01 Arrival To Almaty

  • Welcome to Almaty – City of a thousand colours! After clearing custom & immigration procedures followed by baggage collection, you will be met by your guide in the arrival’s hall. You will be escorted to your vehicle. Water will be served on board your vehicle.
  • Transfer Airport-Hotel by PVT vehicle.
  • Check-in to the Hotel and relax.
  • Overnight at hotel.

Day-02 Almaty City Tour

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • 10:00 a.m. start your day at Panfilov Park, a picturesque green space in the heart of Almaty. Explore the park's charming walking paths and head to the Zenkov Cathedral, a stunning Russian Orthodox Church made entirely of wood. The cathedral is a masterpiece of architecture and provides a great photo opportunity.
  • 02:30 p.m. Lunch time.
  • Continue tour at Republic Square, the main square of Almaty, where you can see the Independence Monument and enjoy the evening ambiance of the city. This is a perfect spot to capture some beautiful sunset photos.
  • 08:00 p.m. Dinner time.
  • Overnight stay in Almaty.
  • 09:30 p.m. Transfer to hotel, Overnight at hotel.

Day-03 Shymbulak Ski Resort

  • Breakfast at hotel.
  • 10:00 a.m. Medeu and Shymbulak – Arrive at Medeu for a walk around, then go to Shymbulak by Cable Car with Spectacular views and mountains and nature.Various fun activities are available (Medeu Ice Skating resort and Ski resort Shymbulak) at Shymbulak that can be booked additionally (not included in the tour price).
  • 03:30 p.m. Lunch time, Shopping tour, 08:00 p.m. Dinner time, 09:30 p.m. Transfer to hotel, Overnight at hotel.

Day-04 Shopping Day + Kok Tobe

  • Breakfast at the hotel, 10:00 Transfer to excursion.
  • Proceed to the Green Bazaar, a vibrant market where locals shop for fresh produce, traditional Kazakh crafts, and souvenirs. Immerse yourself in the bustling atmosphere and try some local snacks.
  • Afterwards, visit Rahat Chocolate Factory, where they present a delightful assortment of chocolates and sweets, each uniquely crafted to please diverse palates. From classic milk chocolates with velvety smoothness to rich dark chocolates with intense cocoa flavors, Rahat offers a variety of options to cater to different tastes.
  • 02:30 p.m. Lunch time.
  • The Kok-Tobe recreational area has a variety of amusement park type attractions and restaurants. It is connected to downtown Almaty by a cable car line. The City Terminal is located near Hotel Kazakhstan. Also, there is a 372 meters tall TV Tower at the foot of the mountain. The tower can be seen from most parts of the city.
  • 08:00 p.m. Dinner time, 09:30 p.m. Transfer to hotel, Overnight at hotel

Day-05 Issyk Lake

  • Enjoy your breakfast at the hotel.
  • 10:00 The Issyk is a picturesque lake located at the height of 1760 meters in the Issyk gorge of Trans-Ili Alatau, around 70 km east of Almaty among beautiful and thick forests along with vast flower fields. The Lake was formed due to a massive rock avalanche some 8000 years ago, which in turn resulted in a 300m high dam. In 1939, development along the lake was heightened only to be destroyed in 1963 by a glacial mudflow.
  • 03:00 p.m. Lunch time, Leisure time before dinner, 08:00 p.m. Dinner time, Overnight at Hotel.

Day-06 Charyn Canyon + Kolsay Lakes

  • Breakfast at the hotel, 08:00 a.m. Transfer to excursion.
  • Charyn Canyon located 200 kilometers away from Almaty. The canyon's depth is 200 meters, and the height of the vertical cliffs reaches 150 to 300 meters. The canyon itself is a natural monument composed of sedimentary rocks with an age of around 12 million years. One of the highlights of the canyon is the so-called "Valley of Castles," which consists of rocky formations resembling towers, as if they were created by unknown giants. The Valley of Castles stretches over 2 kilometers in length, with varying widths ranging from 20 to 80 meters.
  • 03:00 p.m. Lunch time.
  • 04:30 p.m. Kolsay Lakes – one of the most beautiful places in Kazakhstan. These lakes are located at heights 1800, 2250 and 2700 meters above sea level. They are distinguished by the unique mountain watershed consisting of three natural zones and amazing variety of rare plants and animals. Kolsai Lakes are considered a pearl of Northern Tien-Shan located in 300 km from Almaty city.
  • 09:00 p.m. Back to City, Dinner time Overnight at Hotel.

Day-07 Departure Day

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Check out from hotel.
  • Airport transfer.
