Discover The Best Of Eroupe 14 Nights / 15 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in London

  • Fly to London, the capital of England and the United Kingdom.
  • Known for its vibrant culture, cosmopolitan vibe, and rich history.
  • On arrival, meet and greet by our friendly Tour Manager.
  • Transfer to the hotel and assist with smooth check-in.
  • Time to relax and enjoy the hotel’s comfort after your journey.
  • Overnight stay in London.
  • Meals Included: Dinner.

Day 2: Guided City Tour of London – Lord’s Cricket Ground – London Eye

  • Post breakfast, proceed for a guided city tour with our expert local guide.
  • Visit famous London landmarks: (Big Ben, Houses of Parliament, Westminster Abbey, Trafalgar Square, Piccadilly Circus, Tower Bridge, River Thames, Hyde Park and more).
  • Witness the Changing of the Guard at Buckingham Palace (subject to schedule/availability).
  • Visit the Lord’s Cricket Ground – the iconic “Home of Cricket".
  • Tour behind the scenes: (Victorian Pavilion, World-famous Long Room, Players’ Dressing Rooms, MCC Museum, home to the historic Ashes Urn).
  • Enjoy a delightful lunch at Lord’s, soaking in the legendary cricket ambiance.
  • Later, visit the London Eye – one of the UK’s most popular attractions: (Experience breathtaking panoramic views of London. Towering at 135 meters, it offers a stunning look at the River Thames and the city skyline).
  • Overnight stay in London.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 3: Madame Tussauds – Travel to Paris via Eurostar – Welcome to the City of Lights

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel.
  • Visit the world-famous Madame Tussauds Wax Museum in London: (Explore lifelike wax figures of celebrities, world leaders, and sports icons. A fun and interactive experience with photo opportunities galore!).
  • Board the Eurostar – a high-speed train from London to Paris: (Travel through the Channel Tunnel. Enjoy picturesque views of the English and French countryside. Comfortable, swift, and scenic journey between two iconic cities).
  • Arrive in Paris – the City of Romance, Lights, and Glamour: (Known for its fashion, historic monuments, museums, and cabarets).
  • Check-in and relax at your hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Paris.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 4: Guided City Tour of Paris – Eiffel Tower (3rd Level) – Visit Palace of Versailles

  • After breakfast, begin your guided city tour of Paris.
  • Explore some of the city’s most iconic landmarks: (Place Vendôme. Place de l’Opéra Garnier. Musée d’Orsay. Place de la Concorde. Champs-Élysées. Arc de Triomphe. Alexander Bridge. Les Invalides and many more).
  • Visit the Eiffel Tower – ascend to the 3rd (Top) Level for spectacular panoramic views of Paris.
  • Note: Access to the 3rd level is subject to operational status. If unavailable, the visit will be made to the 2nd level instead.
  • Proceed to the Palace of Versailles, a world-renowned symbol of French grandeur: (Located 19 km west of Paris. A UNESCO-listed masterpiece of 17th-century French architecture and art. Explore the Palace, Gardens, and Park – visited by over 15 million people annually. Managed by the French Ministry of Culture).
  • Return to the hotel and relax.
  • Overnight stay in Paris.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 5: Disneyland® Paris – Seine River Cruise – Paris by Night Tour

  • After breakfast, gear up for a fun-filled day at Disneyland® Paris.
  • Choose between.
  • Disney® Park – Experience five magical lands with: (Fairy-tale attractions, Dazzling live shows, Colorful street parades with Disney Characters).
  • Walt Disney Studios® Park – Explore: (Behind-the-scenes of film production. Thrilling stunt shows. Real movie sets from iconic blockbusters).
  • Later, enjoy a romantic cruise on the Seine River.
  • Admire landmarks such as: (Notre-Dame Cathedral. Eiffel Tower. Louvre Museum. Orsay Museum and more).
  • Pass beneath charming bridges while soaking in the city’s timeless beauty.
  • In the evening, embark on the Paris by Night Tour.
  • Witness the magical glow of the “City of Light” after dark.
  • Sights may include: (Illuminated Eiffel Tower. Notre-Dame Cathedral. Louvre Pyramid. Place de la Concorde. Champs-Élysées. Arc de Triomphe).
  • Return to the hotel.
  • Overnight stay in Paris.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 6: Travel to Brussels – Grand Place – Manneken Pis – Mini-Europe – Transfer to Netherlands

  • After breakfast, check out and drive to Brussels, the capital of Belgium and headquarters of the European Union.
  • Upon arrival, visit the iconic Grand Place: (Considered one of the most beautiful squares in Europe. Surrounded by ornate medieval buildings, including the Town Hall).
  • See the famous Manneken Pis statue: (A charming and symbolic bronze sculpture of a little boy).
  • Continue to Mini-Europe, an impressive miniature park: (Explore over 350 detailed scale models of famous European monuments. Each monument is chosen for its architectural significance and symbolic value. A unique and interactive experience of Europe in miniature).
  • Proceed to the Netherlands after sightseeing.
  • Check-in and relax at your hotel.
  • Overnight stay in the Netherlands.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 7: Netherlands – Keukenhof Gardens or Dutch Village – Amsterdam Canal Cruise – Drive to Germany

  • After breakfast, check out from the hotel and board the coach.
  • From March to 11th May.
  • Visit the world-famous Keukenhof Gardens in Lisse:(Marvel at millions of blooming tulips, daffodils, and hyacinths. One of the largest and most stunning flower gardens in the world).
  • From 12th May onwards.
  • Visit a picturesque Dutch village near Amsterdam, famous for: (Iconic windmills, Traditional wooden houses, Artisan workshops showcasing 18th-century Dutch life and heritage).
  • Continue to Amsterdam – the vibrant capital of the Netherlands.
  • Enjoy a scenic glass-topped canal cruise: (Sail through the city’s charming canals. Admire historic bridges and old merchant houses. Learn about the city’s rich legacy and culture).
  • Later, drive to Germany.
  • Check-in at your hotel in the Frankfurt area.
  • Overnight stay in Germany.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 8: Heidelberg Altstadt – Black Forest – Rhine Falls Boat Ride – Drive to Switzerland

  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel and begin the journey toward Switzerland.
  • First stop: Heidelberg Altstadt (Old Town), located along the scenic Neckar River: (Explore the Market Square, cobbled streets, and traditional architecture. Visit the Church of the Holy Spirit, a striking landmark with medieval charm).
  • Proceed to the Heart of the Black Forest in Germany: (Famous for its evergreen woods and storybook villages. Enjoy a demonstration of traditional cuckoo clock making, a regional specialty).
  • Continue your journey toward Switzerland.
  • Arrive at Rhine Falls, the largest waterfall in Europe: (Experience the thrill of a boat ride to get up close to the powerful, cascading waters. Capture panoramic views and enjoy the breathtaking natural beauty).
  • After the visit, drive onward and check in to your hotel in Central Switzerland.
  • Overnight stay in Central Switzerland.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 9: Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe – Visit Interlaken

  • After breakfast, begin your alpine adventure with a scenic drive to Interlaken: (A charming Swiss town nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. Known for its stunning mountain views and as the base for many alpine excursions).
  • Begin your journey to Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe, one of the most spectacular highlights of the tour: (Travel to Grindelwald Terminal. Board the Eiger Express 3S-Bahn – a state-of-the-art gondola that reaches Eigergletscher station in just 15 minutes. From Eigergletscher, transfer to a cogwheel train that takes you to Jungfraujoch, the highest railway station in Europe at 11,333 ft (3,454 m)).
  • Once at the summit: (Explore the Ice Palace, home to intricate ice sculptures carved by talented artists. Visit the Sphinx Observatory Deck offering 360-degree panoramic views of the Aletsch Glacier – the longest glacier in the Alps. Enjoy views of the eternal snow, dramatic alpine peaks, and icy landscapes).
  • Capture unforgettable memories in this magical world of snow and ice.
  • Return to your hotel in Central Switzerland.
  • Overnight stay in Central Switzerland.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 10: Mt. Titlis – Lucerne Orientation – Lindt Home of Chocolate

  • After breakfast, begin a full day of scenic beauty and Swiss experiences..
  • Excursion to Mt. Titlis – reach the summit at 3,020 meters (9,900 ft) via a series of cable cars: (Ride the Rotair, the world’s first rotating cable car, offering 360° panoramic views. Enjoy breathtaking sights of snow-covered peaks, deep crevasses, and glaciers. Visit the famous Cliff Walk, Europe’s highest suspension bridge, for a thrilling experience above the alpine cliffs).
  • Lucerne Orientation Tour: (Visit the iconic Lion Monument, a tribute to Swiss Guards. See the Kapellbrücke (Chapel Bridge), a charming covered wooden bridge that’s a symbol of Lucerne. Stroll through Lucerne's beautiful lakeside setting and old town streets).
  • Visit to Lindt Home of Chocolate: (Discover the world of Swiss chocolate at this interactive attraction. Explore the chocolate fountain, exhibits, and enjoy tastings. Learn about the history, production, and secrets behind Lindt’s signature creations).
  • Return to your hotel in Central Switzerland.
  • Overnight stay in Central Switzerland.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 11: Vaduz – Swarovski Crystal Museum – Innsbruck (Austria)

  • After breakfast, begin your scenic drive through the Alps towards Austria.
  • Stop at Vaduz, capital of the Principality of Liechtenstein: (Take a guided mini train ride through the picturesque town. Discover the serene natural beauty and learn about one of the world’s smallest independent nations. Enjoy panoramic views of the Alpine surroundings and the princely atmosphere of Vaduz).
  • Visit Swarovski Crystal Museum in Wattens: (Explore the magical World of Crystals, featuring dazzling crystal art installations. Discover Swarovski’s century-old craftsmanship, creativity, and innovation).
  • Continue to Innsbruck, the Tyrolean capital of Austria: (Orientation tour of the city known for its blend of imperial history and alpine beauty. Admire the Golden Roof (Goldenes Dachl) – a landmark with fire-gilded copper tiles. Stroll through Maria Theresien Strasse, lined with baroque buildings, boutiques, and charming cafés. Enjoy the beautiful Alpine backdrop, creating a postcard-worthy experience).
  • Check-in to your hotel in Innsbruck / Seefeld.
  • Overnight stay in Innsbruck / Seefeld.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 12: Welcome to Venice – The Enchanting Floating City

  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel and begin your journey to Venice, Italy.
  • Travel through scenic landscapes en route to the romantic city of canals.
  • Arrive at the Venice pier and board a private boat to St. Mark’s Square.
  • At St. Mark’s Square, explore the iconic landmarks: (St. Mark’s Basilica – a stunning masterpiece of Byzantine architecture. Bell Tower – offering panoramic views over Venice (external view). Clock Tower – features zodiac signs and current moon phases. Bridge of Sighs – a symbolic limestone structure linking the Doge’s Palace to the old prison).
  • Enjoy a magical gondola ride: (Ride in a traditional flat-bottomed Venetian rowing boat. Glide along scenic canals framed by baroque buildings and historic bridges. Embrace the romantic atmosphere unique to Venice).
  • Return by private boat to the pier after sightseeing.
  • Proceed to your hotel in Padova / Bologna / Ferrara and complete the check-in.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Padova / Bologna / Ferrara.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 13: Florence – Cradle of the Renaissance & Pisa – The Iconic Leaning Tower

  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel and begin your journey to Florence, the capital of Tuscany and birthplace of the Renaissance.
  • Enjoy a scenic drive through the Italian countryside.
  • Guided City Tour of Florence with an English-speaking local expert: (View the Duomo (Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore) – Florence’s most iconic landmark. Admire the Campanile (Bell Tower) and the Baptistery, famous for the gilded Gates of Paradise. Stroll through Piazza della Signoria, an open-air museum filled with Renaissance sculptures. See the Palazzo Vecchio, Florence's historic town hall. Visit the Ponte Vecchio, Florence’s charming medieval bridge lined with shops).
  • After Florence, continue to Pisa, one of Italy’s most recognized destinations.
  • At Pisa, visit the Square of Miracles (Piazza dei Miracoli): (View the world-famous Leaning Tower of Pisa. Capture memories at this marvel of medieval engineering and architecture).
  • Proceed to your hotel in the Tuscany region and check in.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Tuscany.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 14: Rome – The Eternal City & Vatican City – Spiritual and Historical Marvels

  • After breakfast, check out from your hotel and begin your journey to Rome, the capital of Italy and one of the world’s most historically rich cities.
  • Begin with an orientation tour of Rome, a city layered in history and architectural grandeur.
  • Visit the Vatican City – the world’s smallest independent country: (Enter St. Peter’s Basilica, one of the holiest Catholic shrines, adorned with Michelangelo’s Pietà and Bernini’s awe-inspiring altar. Visit the Sistine Chapel (subject to ticket availability and waiting times), renowned for Michelangelo’s "The Last Judgement" – the conclave site where Popes are elected. Note: Sleeveless tops and shorts are not allowed inside the Vatican. Please dress appropriately).
  • Drive past and stop for views of the Colosseum – the iconic Roman amphitheater famed for gladiatorial battles (entry included only if Vatican tickets are not available).
  • Visit the Trevi Fountain: (Toss a coin over your shoulder and make a wish – legend says it guarantees your return to Rome!).
  • See more of Rome’s legendary landmarks: (The Victor Emmanuel Monument (Altar of the Fatherland). The ancient ruins of the Roman Forum. Many other significant monuments that reflect Rome’s glorious imperial past).
  • Note: Entry to the Vatican Museums & Sistine Chapel may include a waiting time of approx. 1.5 hours due to Jubilee year crowds. In case tickets are unavailable, the Tour Manager will arrange for entry to the Colosseum instead.
  • Check-in and relax at your hotel in Rome.
  • Overnight stay at the hotel in Rome.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner.

Day 15: Departure – Fly Back Home

  • As your incredible European journey comes to an end, it’s time to bid farewell to the stunning landscapes, historic cities, and wonderful friendships you’ve made along the way. After breakfast, complete your check-out process and proceed to the airport for your return flight home. As you board your flight, carry with you a treasure trove of unforgettable experiences and cherished memories from your holiday across Europe.
  • Meals Included: Breakfast.
