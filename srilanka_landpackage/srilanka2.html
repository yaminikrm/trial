Discover The Best Of Srilanka 4 Nights / 5 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival – Pinnawala – Kandy

  • Route: Bandaranaike International Airport → Pinnawala → Kandy, Meals: Dinner, Overnight Stay: Hotel in Kandy.
  • Meet & Greet At Airport
  • Warm welcome by our representative at Bandaranaike International Airport.
  • Proceed towards Kandy.
  • Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage: (Established to care for orphaned elephants, Watch elephants bathe in the river (twice daily), Bottle-feeding of baby elephants (under 3 years)).
  • Explore Spice & Herbal Garden: (Learn about traditional spices: cinnamon, cardamom, nutmeg, pepper, and mace, Experience optional ayurvedic massage treatments using herbal oils).
  • Proceed To Kandy.
  • Visit The Temple Of The Sacred Tooth Relic: (A unesco world heritage site, Houses the sacred tooth relic of lord buddha).
  • Visit Lord Kataragama (Kadiragaman) Temple: (Dedicated to lord murugan (kartikeya), the hindu god of war, Significant religious and historical site).
  • Kandy City Tour: (Kandy Viewpoint – Panoramic city view from the highest point, Gem Museum – Learn about sri lanka’s gem industry, Cultural Show – Enjoy traditional sri lankan music, drumming, and dance).
  • Dinner & Hotel Check-In: (Enjoy a relaxing dinner, Overnight stay at a hotel in kandy).

Day 02: Kandy – Nuwara Eliya

  • Breakfast At The Hotel: (Begin your journey from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya after breakfast).
  • Kandy City Tour (If Not Covered On Day 01): (Gem Museum – Learn about Sri Lanka’s gem origins, rare minerals, and fossils from the Cambrian era, Art And Craft Center – Explore traditional local crafts, Upper Lake Drive – Scenic boat ride across Kandy Lake, Local Market – Shop for souvenirs, spices, and local produce).
  • Visit Peradeniya Botanical Garden: (Sri Lanka’s oldest formal garden with over 4,000 plant species, Famous for orchids, spices, medicinal plants, and palm-lined avenues, Spans over 147 acres and managed by the Department of Agriculture).
  • Visit Bhakta Hanuman Temple: (Located in Ramboda and linked to the Ramayana legend, Believed to be a resting place of Lord Hanuman during his search for Sita, Nearby village "Ravana Goda" reflects the Ramayana influence).
  • Visit Ramboda Falls: (A stunning natural waterfall formed by Ramboda Oya, Located along the scenic Kandy–Nuwara Eliya road).
  • Visit Tea Factory And Tea Plantation: (Explore lush tea estates of Nuwara Eliya, Learn about tea processing and taste fresh Ceylon tea, Ideal spot for photography and cultural immersion).
  • Nuwara Eliya City Tour: (Sri Lanka’s Oldest Post Office – Iconic red-brick colonial building, Colonial Buildings – Remnants of British architecture, St. Xavier’s Church – Historical Roman Catholic church, Victoria Park – Well-maintained botanical garden in the city center, Nuwara Eliya Race Course – Famous for horse racing during the season, Lake Gregory – Picturesque lake ideal for paddle boating and water sports).
  • Dinner And Hotel Check-In: (Enjoy dinner and relax at your hotel in Nuwara Eliya).

Day 03: Nuwara Eliya – Bentota

  • Breakfast At The Hotel: (Begin your journey from Nuwara Eliya to Bentota after a hearty breakfast).
  • Visit Sita Amman Temple: (Believed to be the site where Sita Devi was held captive by King Ravana, A stream near the temple is said to have catered to her needs during captivity, Hanuman's footprints are believed to be visible in the area, Historical discovery of three idols in the stream about a century ago, The soil here is dark, believed to have been scorched by Hanuman).
  • Explore Ashok Vatika (Ashokavanam): (The legendary garden where Sita Devi chose to stay rather than Ravana’s palace, Sheltered by a sacred Ashoka tree offering her comfort during captivity, Believed to have been partially destroyed by Hanuman during his search for Sita, The site remains an important part of the Ramayana trail in Sri Lanka).
  • Optional Stop: White Water Rafting In Kitulgala (Time and interest permitting): (Located between Kandy and Nuwara Eliya in a lush rainforest setting, Enjoy thrilling rafting on the Kelani River, Popular for activities like stream sliding, jungle trekking, bird watching, and cave exploration, Known as the filming location of the 1957 film Bridge on the River Kwai).
  • Visit Turtle Hatchery In Bentota: (Conservation center aimed at protecting endangered sea turtles, See species like the green turtle, leatherback, and olive ridley, Learn about the turtle conservation process from egg collection to release, Opportunity to witness and interact with young turtles).
  • Dinner And Hotel Check-In: (Arrive in Bentota, enjoy a relaxing dinner, and check in to your hotel, Overnight stay at a hotel in Bentota).

Day 04: Bentota – City Area – Colombo

  • Breakfast At The Hotel: (Start the day with breakfast at your hotel in Bentota).
  • Bentota City Tour: (Explore the scenic coastal town of Bentota).
  • Optional Water Sports Activities (Time and interest permitting).
  • Enjoy thrilling water sports on Bentota Beach, Aturuwella, and Bentota River.
  • Activities include: (River cruises, Diving and snorkeling, Water skiing and speed boating, Kitesurfing and windsurfing, Banana boating and tubing).
  • Facilities available at Bentota Water Sports Centre and River Adventures.
  • Madhu River Safari: (Take a boat ride on the scenic Madu River, Spot wildlife such as water monitors, crocodiles, and water snakes, Observe birds like blue-eyed cormorants and fruit bats, Witness local fishermen and playful monkeys along the riverbanks, Enjoy the serene beauty of mangroves and floating islets).
  • Proceed To Colombo: (Travel from Bentota to Colombo, Afternoon at leisure to relax or explore on your own).
  • Dinner And Hotel Check-In: (Arrive in Colombo, enjoy a delicious dinner, and check in to your hotel, Overnight stay at a hotel in Colombo).

Day 05: Colombo – Airport

  • Breakfast At The Hotel: (Enjoy your final breakfast in Sri Lanka before check-out).
  • Colombo City Tour (En Route To The Airport).
  • Explore key attractions in the commercial capital of Sri Lanka: (Galle Face Green, Hindu kovil, Viharamahadevi Park, National Museum, Twin World Trade Center towers, Old Parliament Building, Fort district, Independence Hall at Independence Square, Prominent places of worship: Buddhist, Hindu, Islamic, and Christian).
  • (Entrance fees to sites are not included).
  • Shopping In Colombo (Time permitting).
  • Visit popular shopping destinations: (Odel, House of Fashion, Beverly Street, Majestic City, Noritake showroom).
  • Transfer To Bandaranaike International Airport: (Proceed to the airport for your departure flight, End of a memorable Sri Lankan tour).
