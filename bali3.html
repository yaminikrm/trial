Discover The Best Of Bali 6 Nights / 7 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali – Welcome & Relaxation

  • Arrival: Pick up at the airport with a traditional flower garland welcome.
  • Lunch: Transfer to an Indian restaurant for lunch.
  • Hotel Check-In: Proceed with the check-in process at your hotel and enjoy some leisure time.
  • Evening: Enjoy a delicious dinner at a local Indian restaurant.(Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner)

Day 2: Full-Day Kintamani Volcano & Cultural Tour

  • Morning: After breakfast, depart at 09:00 AM for a full-day exploration..
  • Batik Art: Visit Batubulan Village to see traditional handmade Batik painting.
  • Artisan Villages: Stop at Mas Village to witness painting and wood carving craftsmanship.
  • Coffee Experience: Visit a highland coffee plantation to learn about various types of Indonesian coffee, including the renowned Luwak Coffee. Enjoy a coffee-tasting session on site.
  • Kintamani Highlights: Admire the stunning panoramic view of Mount Batur and Lake Batur.
  • Lunch: Savor an Indonesian lunch at a local restaurant with scenic views.
  • Tegalalang Rice Terraces: On the way back, stop at the iconic Tegalalang Village to see the picturesque rice terraces.
  • Ubud Handicraft Village: Visit Ubud Handicraft Village for local shopping opportunities—ideal for souvenirs and Balinese art collections.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant.(Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner).

Day 3: Water Sports & Uluwatu Sunset Temple Tour

  • Morning: After breakfast, depart at 09:00 AM for an exciting day at Tanjung Benoa Beach.
  • Water Sports: Enjoy a round of Banana Boat ride (included).Optional water sports such as parasailing, jet ski, flying fish, snorkeling, sky tube, sea walker, or diving are available on-site at your own expense.
  • Lunch: Head to a nearby Indian restaurant for lunch.
  • Uluwatu Temple Tour: Visit the iconic Uluwatu Temple, an ancient Hindu temple perched on a cliff over 100 meters above the Indian Ocean.
  • Marvel at the dramatic coastal views and the crashing waves below.
  • Watch out for the sacred monkeys believed to guard the temple.
  • Catch the breathtaking sunset—a highlight of this spiritual site.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant.(Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner)

Day 4: Temple Tour – Taman Ayun, Lake Beratan & Tanah Lot

  • Morning: After breakfast, depart at 09:00 AM for a full-day cultural and scenic temple tour.
  • Taman Ayun Temple: Visit the Taman Ayun Royal Family Temple in Mengwi Village, a beautiful example of traditional Balinese architecture surrounded by serene gardens and moats.
  • Ulun Danu Beratan Temple: Continue to Ulun Danu Beratan Temple in Bedugul, a stunning Hindu temple on Lake Beratan, known for its peaceful setting and photogenic lakeside backdrop.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a traditional Indonesian lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Tanah Lot Temple: Visit the iconic Tanah Lot Temple, uniquely situated on a rocky outcrop in the ocean. Witness the temple’s mystical silhouette against the sea—especially stunning during high tide and sunset.
  • Evening: Dine at a local Indian restaurant.(Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner).

Day 5: Eastern Bali Exploration – Lempuyang Temple & Tirta Gangga

  • Early Morning: After breakfast, depart at 7:30 AM for an unforgettable Eastern Bali adventure.
  • Lempuyang Temple (Heaven’s Gate): Visit the majestic Lempuyang Temple, located on the slopes of Mount Lempuyang, 600 meters above sea level.
  • This sacred Hindu site, known as the "Gateway to Heaven," offers breathtaking views and is a popular photo spot with panoramic backdrops among the clouds.
  • Tirta Gangga Water Palace: Continue to Tirta Gangga, the former royal palace of the Karangasem Kingdom.
  • Enjoy an Indonesian lunch at a local restaurant on-site.
  • Explore the beautiful traditional water garden, fountains, and serene koi-filled ponds set amidst green hills and rice paddies.
  • Evening: Check-in to your hotel in Seminyak. Enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant. (Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner)

Day 6: Full-Day Nusa Penida Island (West Island Tour)

  • Morning: (After breakfast, transfer to Sanur Beach to catch a public fast boat to Nusa Penida Island. Boat departs at 07:30 AM / 08:30 AM and arrives at Toyapakeh Pier around 09:30 AM. Fast boat round-trip tickets included).
  • Tour Highlights (West Nusa Penida): Angel’s Billabong: A natural infinity pool offering a surreal, scenic view—perfect for photos and soaking in the raw beauty of the island. Broken Beach (Pantai Pasih Uug): A unique coastal formation from a collapsed cave, now a picturesque natural arch and lagoon.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a complimentary Indonesian set lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Kelingking Beach (Photo Stop Only): Visit the famous Kelingking Cliff Viewpoint, often compared to a T-Rex head formation.(Note: Descent to the beach and swimming is prohibited for safety reasons).
  • Crystal Bay: Head to Crystal Bay, known for its crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life—perfect for swimming or snorkeling (bring swimwear).
  • Return: Depart from Buyuk Pier by fast boat back to Bali.
  • Evening: Arrive at Sanur Beach and enjoy dinner at a local Indian restaurant.(Meals Included: Lunch, Dinner) Note: Vehicles used on Nusa Penida Island will be Suzuki APV, Avanza, or Xenia (subject to availability).

Day 7: Departure from Bali

  • Morning: Enjoy breakfast at the hotel.
  • Free Time: Relax or explore nearby areas at your own pace.
  • Departure: Transfer to Ngurah Rai International Airport for your onward flight.(No meals included today).
