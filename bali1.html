Discover The Best Of Bali 4 Nights / 5 Days

Day Wise Itinerary

Day 01: Arrival in Bali – Hotel Check-in – Indian Lunch & Dinner

  • Arrival at Bali International Airport.
  • Warm welcome by our local representative.
  • Private transfer to the hotel for check-in.
  • Time to freshen up and relax after your journey.
  • Enjoy a delicious Indian lunch at a local restaurant.
  • Afternoon at leisure – relax at the hotel or explore nearby areas on your own.
  • In the evening, proceed for a sumptuous Indian dinner at a popular local restaurant.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 2: Melasti Beach – GWK Cultural Park – Uluwatu Temple – Kecak Dance

  • Breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit Melasti Beach: (Famous for limestone cliffs and turquoise waters, Ideal for photography and relaxation).
  • Explore GWK Cultural Park: (See the massive Lord Vishnu riding Garuda statue, Enjoy cultural and artistic exhibits).
  • Visit Uluwatu Temple: (Located on a cliff with stunning ocean views, One of Bali's most iconic sea temples).
  • Watch the Kecak Dance performance at sunset: (Traditional Balinese fire and chant dance, Set against a dramatic ocean backdrop).
  • Enjoy Indian lunch and dinner at well-rated local restaurants.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Nusa Penida Island – West Tour Highlights

  • Early breakfast at the hotel.
  • Proceed to Sanur Harbour for fast boat transfer to Nusa Penida Island.
  • Full-day West Nusa Penida tour, covering major highlights
  • Angel’s Billabong – Natural infinity pool with crystal-clear waters.
  • Broken Beach – A scenic coastal arch with panoramic ocean views.
  • Kelingking Beach – Iconic cliff formation resembling a T-Rex, offering breathtaking views.
  • Crystal Bay – Known for its calm, clear waters and snorkeling opportunities.
  • Enjoy a simple local lunch on the island.
  • Return to Bali by fast boat in the evening.
  • Head straight to a local Indian restaurant for a delicious dinner.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 04: Cultural & Scenic Bali – Ubud Exploration

  • Enjoy breakfast at the hotel.
  • Visit Celuk & Mas Village: (Famous for Balinese silver jewelry and wood carvings).
  • Explore Tegenungan Waterfall: (Located in a lush jungle setting; ideal for nature lovers and photographers).
  • Head to the Kintamani Highlands: (Enjoy stunning views of Mount Batur Volcano and Lake Batur).
  • Discover the Tegalalang Rice Terraces: (Known for stepped rice fields and scenic green landscapes).
  • Visit the Ubud Royal Palace: (Historical residence of Ubud’s royal family).
  • Shop at the Ubud Art Market: (Popular for local handicrafts, souvenirs, and artwork).
  • Stop at a local coffee plantation: (Learn about traditional coffee-making and sample Luwak coffee).
  • Enjoy Indian lunch and dinner at selected Indian restaurants.
  • Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Departure – Transfer to Airport

  • Enjoy your final breakfast at the hotel.
  • Complete check-out formalities.
  • Transfer to Bali International Airport (Denpasar) by private vehicle.
  • End of tour with cherished memories of Bali’s culture, nature, and warm hospitality.
